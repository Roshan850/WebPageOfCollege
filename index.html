About RDEC

Established in 2006, R.D. Engineering College (RDEC) is affiliated with AKTU and approved by AICTE. Located on NH-58, Duhai, Ghaziabad, RDEC is committed to delivering quality education and preparing students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

Departments

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

Focuses on modern computing, AI, cybersecurity, and software engineering.

Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)

Specializes in VLSI, communication, and embedded systems.

Mechanical Engineering (ME)

Expertise in thermodynamics, robotics, and manufacturing processes.

Civil Engineering (CE)

Strength in construction, structural engineering, and sustainability.

Computer Science & Engineering

The CSE Department offers top-notch education with modern labs, ITCRC, and strong industry collaborations. Training includes cutting-edge domains like AI, ML, IoT, and Big Data.
