About RDEC

R.D. Engineering College (RDEC), established in 2006 by the Rameshwar Dayal Educational Trust, is situated on Delhi–Meerut Road, Duhai, Ghaziabad. Affiliated to AKTU and approved by AICTE, RDEC offers a dynamic and industry-aligned learning environment with modern infrastructure and strong placement support.

Departments

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

B.Tech (120 seats), M.Tech (18 seats) – established in 2006 with innovative pedagogy and strong industry links.

Computer Science & Engineering Department

The CSE Department offers cutting-edge education supported by modern labs, ITCRC, industrial training, and soft-skill enhancement programs.
